BloxPro is a discord bot that provides service for Roblox groups, connecting both together seamlessly. It has features such as Rank Management, Auto Promotions, Medals, Quotas, Terminate, Suspend and much more.

This is the documentation that is here to help you use BloxPro and get familiar with it.

Join the [discord server](https://discord.gg/Gcndmxe3QC), here you can get support and ask other BloxPro users questions.
