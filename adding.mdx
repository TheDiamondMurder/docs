For the bot to function, you will obviously have to add it to your server. You can do so with [this link](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=940334064009895986\&permissions=8\&integration_type=0\&scope=bot) or you can join our [discord server](https://discord.gg/Gcndmxe3QC) and run **/invite** in the bot commands channel

After that the bot is in your discord server and you are ready to move onto the next step
