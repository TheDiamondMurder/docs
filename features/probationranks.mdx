---
title: "Probation Ranks"
description: "How Probation Ranks work"
---

Probation Ranks stop users from advancing to a rank automatically with XP and will not allow them to rank up until they are manually ranked to/past that rank with commands such as /promote or /setrank

![image.png](/images/image.png)

To set a probation rank

```
/rank probation [Rank ID] [True or False]
```
