---
title: "Records System"
description: "The Records System"
---

The Records System allows you to record any negative behaviour from your members in your group, you can log them for anything you want and their records can be seen in their profile in **/profile**

To log a user

```
/record add [Username] [Record Reason]
```

However, if you want to remove a record

```
/record remove [Username] [Record Number]
```

Their record number as well as their other records can be seen in **/profile**
