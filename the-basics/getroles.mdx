---
title: "Updating your Roles"
description: "How to update your roles"
---

If your roles are outdated, you can simply use the **/getroles** command, this will update your roles and your nickname to the latest, and will give you the roles for your current rank, and groups you are in.

```
/getroles
```

<Warning>
  You must be verified to use this command
</Warning>
