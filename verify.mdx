description: "Description of your new file."

To link your Roblox group to your server, you need to be verified. This is no complicated task, just run **/verify** in any server with BloxPro in it. You will be guided on how to verify. You will have to put a code in your about me page on Roblox, after you have done this, you will have linked your Roblox account to BloxPro.
